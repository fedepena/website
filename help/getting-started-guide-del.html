Getting Started Guide

Dissecting the Demo Company

The purpose of this guide is to show you how to start using myAbakus to make it valuable for your company. To do this we will show you step by step where the information you can see in the demo company comes from. The demo company gives you a good idea of what an account looks like with data. But it does not tell you how the information was entered or what is the best way to do it. That is why we have created this guide: so you can see the process step by step and apply it in the evaluation and implementation of the software.
